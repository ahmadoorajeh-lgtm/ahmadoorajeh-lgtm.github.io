Meta ads for 15 locations

"Every dollar tracked, every location covered."

Client: Northstar Preschools Stack: Meta Ads Manager · Google Ads · weekly scorecards Result: CPL 28% under target

Problem Fifteen locations, one budget, no clear picture

A preschool network with 15 locations across two states needed enrolment leads in every market. Spend was split across locations with very different audiences and competition, and without honest per-location reporting, budget kept flowing to the loudest campaign instead of the best one.

Build Campaigns run end to end, judged by one number

  • Full campaign management. Strategy, audiences, creatives, and budgets for every location, on Meta and Google, run as one coordinated programme rather than 15 separate experiments.
  • A weekly scorecard. Every location's spend, leads, and cost per lead, side by side, with week-over-week movement. Winners get budget, underperformers get fixed or paused.
  • Tracking that tells the truth. Validated lead counting across every campaign type (see the invisible leads), so decisions run on complete data.
The result

Cost per lead of $53.72 against a $75 target, the best in eight weeks, at flat spend. The strongest locations identified and fed, the weak ones fixed instead of funded.
