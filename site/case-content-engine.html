The monthly content engine

"A month of on-brand content, delivered like clockwork."

Client: Northstar Preschools Stack: Canva · brand kit · content calendar · video editing Result: 14+ posts/month, 11 locations

Problem Eleven locations, one brand, no rhythm

Each location needed a steady social presence: three posts a week mixing educational, fun, and marketing content. Left to ad-hoc posting, the brand looked different at every location, key dates got missed, and some pages went quiet for weeks.

Build Plan once, produce everything, deliver ready-to-post

  • A monthly content calendar. Every post planned around awareness days, seasonal moments, and the brand's own events. Recurring anchors (newsletter, employee of the month) land in the same slot every month.
  • All creative produced in one pass. Branded graphics, carousels, and reels designed in Canva against the brand kit: same colours, same fonts, same illustrated style, every time. Video clips edited into ready-to-post reels.
  • A delivery workflow, not just files. Each location's team gets their posts mapped to dates, with captions written and posting reminders scheduled. Compliance is tracked weekly so quiet pages get caught early.
The result

14+ branded posts produced every month across 11 locations: planned, designed, captioned, and delivered ready to post. One consistent brand everywhere, and no more quiet pages.
