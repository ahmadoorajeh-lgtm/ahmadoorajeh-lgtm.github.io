The local SEO push

"Pages built to own the local search results."

Client: Northstar Preschools Stack: WordPress · Elementor · Rank Math · keyword research Result: 17+ optimised pages live

Problem Invisible where parents actually search

When a parent searches "preschool near me" or "daycare in [their town]", the results page is the shortlist. The brand's site wasn't on it: competitors held those positions with dedicated local pages, while the brand had one generic site trying to cover every market at once.

Build One page per search, built to beat what's ranking

  • Keyword research per market. Mapped what parents in each town actually type, then prioritised the searches with real enrolment intent behind them.
  • Pages engineered against the competition. Before writing, studied the pages currently ranking and built each new one to be more useful, more local, and better structured than what's there.
  • A repeatable publishing rulebook. Consistent structure, on-page optimisation via Rank Math, internal linking, and location-specific detail on every page, published on WordPress with Elementor. 17+ pages and counting.
The result

17+ keyword-targeted pages written and published across two states, each built against the pages currently ranking. The brand now competes for its local searches instead of sitting them out.
