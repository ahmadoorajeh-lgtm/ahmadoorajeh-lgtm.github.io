The invisible leads

"Found the leads the reports were missing, then made sure they never went missing again."

Client: Northstar Preschools Stack: Meta Ads API · Apps Script · Google Sheets Result: accurate CPL, full-funnel visibility

Problem The numbers said the ads were failing. They weren't.

Meta messaging campaigns were generating real leads: people starting conversations, asking about enrolment, booking tours. But the reporting script only counted standard lead-form submissions, so every messaging lead was invisible in the numbers.

On paper, cost-per-lead looked far worse than reality. Budget decisions were being made on incomplete data, and the campaigns quietly doing the best work looked like the ones failing.

Build Count everything, then verify it

  • Diagnosed the gap. Compared what the Ads platform showed against what the report captured, campaign by campaign, and isolated exactly which lead actions were being dropped.
  • Rebuilt the reporting script. The new version pulls every lead-type action from the Ads API: form fills, messaging conversations started, calls. Not just the one action type the old script knew about.
  • Validated against platform totals. The rebuilt report was run side by side with the platform's own numbers until they matched, every campaign type, every time.
The result

Full-funnel visibility restored. Accurate cost-per-lead across every campaign type, and previously invisible messaging leads recovered into reporting, permanently.
